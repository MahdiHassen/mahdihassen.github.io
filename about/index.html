this website

this website is pure html & css, no javascript or anything. i do this for a couple of reasons, but mainly due to compatibility, speed and simplicity.
on here i mainly just share things i find interesting. this includes projects im working on, thoughts on what im reading/studying and just things that i wish to keep for reference. i'd like to eventually have a personal knowledge base, similar to something like Andy Matuschak's working notes.
its changed quite a bit since its inception, but i originally based the design of this website on something i saw at www.brutalistwebsites.com. unfortunately, i can't find the original inspiration anymore, but if you know the original website please shoot me an email so i can give them credit.
