me

i'm interested in a lot of things, but generally i love all things computers. things like computer architecture, soc design & embedded systems really peak my interest. outside of engineering i read a lot of philosophy, which i try to talk about in my blog. i also enjoy astronomy and building telescopes.

if you need to get to me, email is the quickest way: mhassen@uwaterloo.ca

this website

this website is pure html & css. i do this for a couple of reasons, but mainly due to compatibility, speed and simplicity.i am also not a web developer, so it's probably fine.

on here i mainly just share things i find interesting. this includes projects im working on, thoughts on what im reading/studying and just things that i wish to keep for reference. i sometimes share small webapps i make for my own use, but i remove them when i no longer have a use for them.

its changed quite a bit since its inception, but i originally based the design of this website on something i saw at www.brutalistwebsites.com. unfortunately, i can't find the original inspiration anymore, but if you know the original website please shoot me an email so i can give them credit.

you can navigate this site by pressing/clicking the names of the pages. For example, in the astrophotography section, pressing the header "../astrophotography" will send you to the home page.
